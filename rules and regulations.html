Hostel Management System

Hostel Rules and Regulations

  • Quiet hours must be observed from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM.
  • No guests are allowed in the hostel rooms after 10:00 PM.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited within the hostel premises.
  • Residents are responsible for keeping their rooms clean and tidy.
  • Common areas should be used responsibly and kept clean.
  • Respectful behavior towards fellow residents and staff is mandatory.
  • Violation of rules may result in disciplinary action.
  • Voilance with the fellow mates is strictly prohibited.
  • All leave applications or any permission should be taken by the warden before commitment.
